FAQ

These are the most frequently asked questions by tourists.

  1. What are the best seasons to visit Sri Lanka?
    • Generally, the best time to visit the west and south coasts as well as the Cultural Triangle is between December and April, while on the east coast the climate is at its finest from May through to October.

  2. What is the cost to visit Sri lanka?
    • The average price of a 7-day trip to Sri Lanka is $1,627 for a solo traveler, $2,899 for a couple, and $1,473 for a family of 4. Sri Lanka hotels range from $32 to $207 per night with an average of $59, while most vacation rentals will cost $40 to $400 per night for the entire home.

  3. What is Sri Lanka's Covid 19 Pandemic situation?
    • Currently there can be seen 400 to 500 cases being reported perday. But the situation has been handled by the goverment by taking certain actions and vaccination programms are on.

  4. What to do in Srilanka?
    • Sri Lanka is famous for its rich biodiversity, large cinnamon and tea exportation, and stunning natural wonders! It's also known as the Pearl of the Indian Ocean and has diverse cultural and religious elements across the nation.
